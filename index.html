Avnet Services is a global organization comprised of IT, lifecycle, business and education experts that collaborates with customers, partners, integrators and suppliers to maximize the potential of IT and data systems. We address complex business needs with advanced technology solutions to accelerate growth. Avnet Services is a single-source provider of enterprise software, hardware and services -- from procurement through integration, operations, management and disposal

Ignite 2014 aims to share and showcase best practices by DEV and QA in Mobile, Web, Infrastructure projects, solution accelerators, global delivery success stories, and ideas via technical sessions. These sessions are in the form of short, impactful presentations and demonstrations (30 minutes or less). Ignite 2014 also provides a platform to meet the AVNET Services leadership team.
